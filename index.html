how it works

Easy steps for setting up a financial plan

01

Set up an e-meeting

During the meeting, i will ask a questions about your financial goals, family situation, and other relevant information to help you make an informed decision about the best coverage for you. i will also explain the various benefits and features associated with each policy, and provide you with quotes so you can compare and contrast your options.

02

Fill up e-application

Complete the application form.The form will ask you to provide important information such as your contact details, educational background and work experience. Once you have your completed application form, you will need to submit it to your financial agent. Your agent will review the information on the application form and request any additional information they may need. They will then assess your application and determine if you qualify.

03

pay e-premium

Yes, you can pay your e-premium via Visa, Mastercard, bank transfer or Gcash. Please contact your insurance provider for more information on how to pay your e-premium.

04

receive e-policy

Once your policy is fully processed and approved,me your financial agent will provide you with a copy of the policy. I will explain the terms and conditions of the policy, as well as any additional information you will need to know in order to make the most of your policy.
